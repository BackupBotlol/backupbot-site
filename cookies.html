Cookie Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how BackupBot uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (backupbot.net), By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of text that is sent to a user's browser, desktop client or device, The browser provides this piece of text to the device of the originating user when this visitor returns.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access, You cannot opt out of these cookies.

Preference Cookies

  • Dark Mode Preferences: We store your preference for dark mode in a cookie called "theme" which remembers your preferred display setting.
  • Cookie Consent: We use a "cookie-consent" cookie to remember your choice regarding cookie usage on our site.

Analytics Cookies

We do not (currently) use third-party analytics cookies on our website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enable site features and functionality, such as the dark mode toggle.
  • To remember your preferences, such as your preferred theme (light mode or dark mode).
  • To improve user experience by saving you from having to re-enter information or preferences on each visit.

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we also use local storage for similar purposes, Local storage is a technology that allows websites to store information on your device in a way that's similar to cookies, but with some differences in how the data is managed and accessed, We use local storage primarily for:

  • Storing your dark mode preference
  • Remembering your cookie consent decision

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent, The methods for doing so vary from browser to browser, and from version to version, You can obtain information about blocking and deleting cookies via these links:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

note that blocking cookies may impact the functionality of our website, For example, if you disable cookies, you may not be able to use the dark mode feature, and your preference won't be remembered between visits.

Our Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies, If you choose to decline, we will only use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time, We will (or won't) notify users of significant changes through our Discord support server or through direct announcement in your log channel.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, Please note that we do not control cookies used by these websites, We encourage you to read the cookie policies of these websites when you visit them.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy or our data practices or any other questions, please contact us through our Discord support server or through our Email.
